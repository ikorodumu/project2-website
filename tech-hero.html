Tech Hero: Dr. Timnit Gebru

Advocating for Ethical AI and Diversity in Tech

Who is Dr. Timnit Gebru?

Dr. Timnit Gebru is a computer scientist known for her groundbreaking work in **AI ethics, algorithmic bias, and diversity in tech**. As a co-founder of **Black in AI**, she has been a strong advocate for inclusion and equity in the artificial intelligence industry.

She was previously a researcher at Google AI and co-authored a pivotal paper on **bias in large-scale language models**, highlighting the dangers of unregulated AI development. Following her controversial exit from Google, she founded the **Distributed AI Research Institute (DAIR)** to promote independent research in AI ethics.
